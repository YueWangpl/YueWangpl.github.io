Yue Wang

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Stony Brook University, advised by Prof. Xianfeng Gu. My research interests span 3D geometry processing, computer graphics, and generative modeling. I've worked on projects involving mesh generation for simulation, controllable face synthesis, and optimal transport-based remeshing. My work has been published at venues including WACV, SIAM IMR, and IEEE VR.
